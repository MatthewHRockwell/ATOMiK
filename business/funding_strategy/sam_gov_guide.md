# SAM.gov Registration Guide

## Why Register?

Required for all federal grants (SBIR, STTR, CHIPS Act) and contracts.
Must be completed before submitting any federal funding application.

## Prerequisites

- Incorporated entity (LLC or C-Corp)
- EIN (Employer Identification Number)
- Business bank account
- NAICS codes identified

## 8-Step Process

### 1. Create Login.gov Account

- Go to login.gov
- Set up MFA (required)
- Use the founder's email address

### 2. Request UEI (Unique Entity Identifier)

- Replaces the old DUNS number (free)
- Request at sam.gov during registration
- Processing: 1-3 business days

### 3. Gather Required Information

| Item | Details |
|------|---------|
| EIN | From IRS (configured in config.yaml) |
| NAICS codes | 334413 (Semiconductor Manufacturing), 541715 (R&D in Physical Sciences) |
| Banking info | Routing + account number for EFT |
| Annual revenue | $0 (pre-revenue) |
| Employee count | 1 |
| Address | Business address |

### 4. Complete Core Data Section

- Entity information
- General information
- Financial information
- Executive compensation (N/A for sole proprietor)

### 5. Representations & Certifications

- Small business self-certification
- US ownership attestation
- Organizational type
- NAICS size standards

### 6. Submit for Validation

- Review all sections
- Submit electronically
- Print confirmation page

### 7. CAGE Code Assignment

- Commercial and Government Entity code
- Automatically assigned after submission
- Processing: 1-7 business days

### 8. Final Approval

- SAM.gov validates entity information
- Cross-references IRS, state records
- Processing: 10-15 business days
- **Total timeline: 2-4 weeks**

## Annual Renewal

- SAM.gov registration must be renewed annually
- Set calendar reminder for 30 days before expiry
- No cost for renewal
